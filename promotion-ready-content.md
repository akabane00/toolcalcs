### 질문: "How much is $30 an hour annually?"
**답변:**
$30 an hour equals **$62,400 per year** if you work 40 hours per week for 52 weeks (2,080 hours total).

Here's the full breakdown:
- Annual: $62,400
- Monthly: $5,200
- Biweekly: $2,400
- Weekly: $1,200
- Daily (8 hours): $240

After taxes (federal + FICA + state), you'll take home roughly $46,800 to $49,920 depending on your state and filing status.

I wrote a detailed breakdown with tax estimates by state here: https://toolcalcs.com/blog/how-much-is-30-an-hour-annually/

### 질문: "What is a good credit score?"
**답변:**
A good credit score is **670-739** on the FICO scale (used by 90% of lenders). Here's the full breakdown:

- 800-850: Exceptional
- 740-799: Very Good
- 670-739: Good
- 580-669: Fair
- 300-579: Poor

With a "Good" score (670+), you'll qualify for most loans and credit cards with reasonable rates. With "Very Good" (740+), you'll get the best rates available.

The average US credit score is about 715, which falls in the "Good" range.

Full guide with APR differences by score range: https://toolcalcs.com/blog/what-is-a-good-credit-score-2026/

### 질문: "How many ounces are in a gallon?"
**답변:**
There are **128 fluid ounces in a US gallon**.

Quick conversion chart:
- 1 gallon = 128 oz = 4 quarts
- 1 quart = 32 oz = 2 pints
- 1 pint = 16 oz = 2 cups
- 1 cup = 8 oz

Note: UK imperial gallons are different — 1 UK gallon = 160 imperial fluid ounces.

Printable conversion chart: https://toolcalcs.com/blog/how-many-ounces-in-a-gallon/

### 질문: "How do I calculate compound interest?"
**답변:**
The compound interest formula is: **A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)**

Where:
- A = final amount
- P = principal (starting amount)
- r = annual interest rate (as decimal)
- n = number of times compounded per year
- t = number of years

Example: $10,000 at 7% compounded monthly for 10 years:
A = 10,000(1 + 0.07/12)^(12×10) = **$20,097**

Your $10,000 nearly doubles in 10 years!

Free calculator to try different amounts: https://toolcalcs.com/financial-calculators/compound-interest-calculator/
